Search Ctrl + F

AdBooker lists have built in search capabilities.
Press the shortcut (Ctrl + F) or alternatively hover over the bottom right corner for the search bar, click the search button.
AdBooker will remember your search term. To clear a search, clear the search box's value and click search. The search bar will disappear as well
