How to delete a record

Step 1 click the edit button in any details pane

in any details pane near the top next to the client name to the left of the date and publication you should see the edit button clicking it takes you to the edit form.

Step 2 Delete button

click the delete button on the bar at the bottom right

Step 3 give a reason

A delete reason is required. Once its added click delete to remove the record.

Note: the record will be deleted but is still accessible under "records -> deleted"
